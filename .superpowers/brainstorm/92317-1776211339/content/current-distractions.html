What's wrong with this?

  • No profiles — one flat list applies to everything. Can't have different block lists for different types of work.
  • No defaults — new users see empty lists. No guidance on what to block.
  • Sites and apps are separate cards — but they should be grouped per profile (a "Social Media" profile blocks both the apps AND the sites).
  • Always-Relevant is orphaned — it's on the same page but has nothing to do with blocking profiles.
  • No visual indication of which profile is active during a focus session.
