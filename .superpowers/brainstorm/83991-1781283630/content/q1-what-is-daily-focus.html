After the session ends, what happens to "finish the ingest fix"?

A

Just a session name (throwaway)

The text only names the session. It shows on the pill while you work and in session history afterward. No new object anywhere. Simplest possible fix — but the coach can't say "yesterday you said you'd finish the ingest fix" without digging through history, and there's no "today's focus" anywhere on the dashboard.

B

A real "Daily Focus" for today (recommended)

It becomes today's focus — visible on the Today tab and the pill, can have multiple sessions against it during the day, and expires tonight. The coach can reference it all day ("you committed to the ingest fix this morning"). If it turns out to be bigger than a day, one click promotes it to a Weekly Goal. This is the "morning commitment" from your parent-teen core loop.

C

Still a Weekly Goal, but cleaned up

Keep creating Weekly Goals but mark them "ad-hoc" so they auto-archive at week's end and don't clutter the Goals tab. Least new machinery — but "idk" still becomes a goal, and a daily commitment masquerading as a weekly goal is exactly the confusion we're trying to kill.
