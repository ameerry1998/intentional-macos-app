Frequently asked questions

Does it work with Safari / Arc / Firefox?
Yes. The native macOS app monitors all browsers. A Chrome/Edge extension adds deeper in-browser features, but blocking and focus monitoring work everywhere out of the box.
What if I need YouTube for work?
That's the whole point. The AI scores each page against your current task. A React tutorial during a coding block? Allowed. MrBeast compilation? Blocked. Same site, different verdicts.
Can I really not turn it off?
With Strict Mode + accountability partner: the app survives force-quit, relaunches on boot, and requires a 6-digit code texted to your partner to disable. You chose this.
Is my data private?
100%. AI scoring runs entirely on your Mac using Apple's built-in models. No browsing data ever leaves your device. The only network call is optional partner sync.
Does it slow down my Mac?
No. Uses ~50MB RAM and near-zero CPU when you're focused. AI scoring is milliseconds via on-device models. Tab polling every 10 seconds, not continuously.
How is this different from Freedom / Cold Turkey?
Those are blocklists — block everything or nothing. Intentional uses AI to understand context, rewards focus with earned browse time, and escalates progressively instead of hard-blocking immediately.
