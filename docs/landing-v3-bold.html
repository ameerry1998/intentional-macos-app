FAQ

Will this actually stop me? +

Strict mode survives force-quit, relaunches on login, and needs your accountability partner's code to disable. You'd have to uninstall the entire app — and your partner gets notified. So yeah, it stops you.

Does the AI send my data anywhere? +

No. All AI runs on-device using Apple's Foundation Models or a local Qwen3-4B model via MLX. Your browsing data never leaves your Mac. We literally cannot see what you do.

What if I need social media for work? +

The AI understands context. If your focus block is "Social media marketing" and you're on Instagram, it scores that as relevant. You won't get nudged. It's not a dumb blocklist — it actually understands what you're supposed to be doing.

Can I use it without an accountability partner? +

Yes. The earned browse system, AI scoring, progressive enforcement, and scheduling all work standalone. The accountability partner and strict mode are optional layers for when you want maximum commitment.

Why not just use Screen Time? +

Screen Time blocks apps by time limit. It doesn't know what you're working on, doesn't reward focus, doesn't escalate progressively, and you can dismiss it with one click. It's a speed bump. Intentional is a system.

What macOS versions are supported? +

macOS 14 (Sonoma) and later. Apple Foundation Models require macOS 26 for on-device AI; older versions use the bundled MLX Qwen3-4B model automatically.
