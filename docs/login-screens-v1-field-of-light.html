Field of light.

Hundreds of warm dots faintly twinkling against the dark, with a slow parallax drift between near and far layers. A subtle warm radial wash at center suggests a horizon. Quiet, infinite, contemplative — and the same on every state, so it becomes the brand.

Star count
~140 for macOS · ~50 for iOS · scale by area (1 star per 6500 px²)
Two layers
60% near · 40% far · independent parallax
Twinkle
3–6s loops · randomized · ease-in-out · scale 1→1.3, opacity → 1.0
Parallax
near 32s ±12px · far 56s ±6px (opposite phase)
Star tints
60% rgba(255,200,150) · 25% rgba(255,160,100) · 15% rgba(255,230,200)
Star size
0.6–1.8pt random · glow box-shadow 0 0 (size×3)pt @ 70%
Reduced motion
isReduceMotionEnabled → freeze parallax + twinkle, keep static field
