Alarms tab · iPhone

Wake & sleep times — set once, applies everywhere.

In the iPhone Alarms tab you set two anchors: when you wake up and when you go to sleep. The wake time fires a real alarm. The sleep time blocks pretty much everything across both devices until your wake time. Configure once on iPhone — it syncs to Mac automatically.

iPhone Alarms tab. Two anchors. Each is a card with time, days, sub-info, and an enable toggle. The Sleep card explicitly tags 📱 iPhone and 💻 Mac so the user knows it's not just a local thing.

What the wake time does

  • Plays an alarm on iPhone — gentle 5-min ramp by default. Tap your puck to dismiss.
  • Ends BEDTIME on both devices at the alarm time. BEDTIME → OFF
  • Does NOT auto-start FOCUS — you ease in. Q2 in the open questions.

What the sleep time does

  • Triggers BEDTIME on both devices. FOCUS or OFF → BEDTIME
  • Blocks pretty much everything on iPhone and Mac — bedtime blocklist (social, news, doom-scroll). Stricter than the focus blocklist on purpose.
  • 30-min wind-down ramp before the actual sleep time — gradual screen dim, soft notification "going to sleep in 28 min".
  • Mac shows wake time in its dashboard hero card and menu bar pill.
  • Auto-ends at your wake time the next morning. No manual untoggle required.

How sync works

Wake and sleep times are account-scoped — stored on the backend, not per-device. Edit on iPhone, Mac picks up within seconds. Same shape as the partner / focus session sync. If you sign in on a new Mac, it inherits your existing wake / sleep automatically.

Edge cases

  • iPhone offline at sleep time — Mac still flips to BEDTIME via its own clock + the synced sleep time. No dependency on iPhone being awake.
  • You stay up past sleep time — Mac & iPhone go to BEDTIME anyway. To override, tap the puck or hit "Stay up 30 min" on either device.
  • You wake up before the alarm — tap puck on iPhone, both devices flip to OFF, alarm cancelled.
  • Different sleep on weekends — set the days per alarm. Default sleep is "every day", but you can split weekday / weekend.
🔄Auto-syncs to Mac
Set wake / sleep on iPhone once — Mac picks it up automatically. The wake / sleep markers show in Mac's schedule view, and they're editable from Mac's Settings → Alarms (the same record). Changing it on either device updates the other within seconds.

Focus interventions

When FOCUS is on, these turn on too.

Each one is opt-out. By default Focus Mode bundles all of them — that's the canonical "deep work" experience. Settings → Interventions lets users toggle individual pieces off if they want a softer / harder shape. The bundle is the default; the toggles are escape hatches.

1 Distractions blocking DEFAULT ON
Hard block of every app + site in your distractions list. Twitter / Instagram / news / TikTok / etc. shut at the OS level. Override is possible from earned-browse pool, otherwise locked.
Setting: on/off · Customize: Distractions list (apps + URLs)
2 Context-switch overlay DEFAULT ON
10-second full-screen countdown when you Cmd-Tab to a non-relevant app during focus. You can wait it out or click "Go back."
Setting: on/off · Customize: countdown duration (5s / 10s / 20s)
3 AI relevance scoring DEFAULT ON
Browser tabs scored against your intention. Off-topic content (even on allowed sites) flagged. Drives the off-task nudges and the screen tint.
Setting: on/off · Customize: strictness (loose / balanced / strict)
4 Screen red shift DEFAULT ON
When AI marks the current content as off-task, the screen gradually shifts red over ~30s. Aversive but not punishing — clears immediately when you return to focus.
Setting: on/off · Customize: intensity (subtle / strong)
5 Off-task nudge toast DEFAULT ON
Small toast in corner: "Drifted to YouTube — back to your work?" Two buttons: stay 30s, or go back. First-tier intervention before red shift escalates.
Setting: on/off · Customize: tone (gentle / direct / sharp)
6 Block start ritual DEFAULT ON
Modal overlay at the start of a scheduled block forces you to type a one-line intention before enforcement engages. The "make me plan" feature.
Setting: on/off · Customize: auto-fill intention from block title (yes / no)
7 Block end ritual DEFAULT ON · brief
3-second card at the end of a block: focus score, total minutes, app breakdown. Auto-dismisses; no input required. Builds the "I just did something" loop.
Setting: on/off · Customize: celebrate >80% with confetti (yes / no)
8 Earned browse mode DEFAULT OFF · opt-in
Earn distraction-time credits while focused. Spend them on otherwise-blocked sites later. Gamifies discipline. Off by default because some users find it gameable.
Setting: on/off · Customize: earn rate (1 min focused → N seconds browse)
9 Always-on pill widget DEFAULT ON
Menu bar pill shows current state, intention, timer, focus score. Always visible during focus so you remember what you're doing.
Setting: on/off · Customize: show focus score (yes / no), pill position
10 Distracting app force-quit DEFAULT OFF · opt-in
Hard mode: when you switch to a distracting app, it gets minimized or quit immediately. No countdown, no overlay — just gone. For users who want firmer guardrails.
Setting: on/off · Customize: minimize vs quit
