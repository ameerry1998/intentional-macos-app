# Intentional Landing Page — Full Description for Review

**Product:** Intentional — a macOS distraction-blocking app for knowledge workers that uses on-device AI to understand work context, progressive enforcement (nudge → grayscale → block), and an earned browse system where focused work earns social media time.

**Target audience:** Developers, writers, founders, grad students — people who do cognitively demanding work on a Mac and struggle with digital distractions.

**Competitors:** Freedom, Cold Turkey, one sec, Screen Time

---

## Visual Design

**Overall aesthetic:** Dark mode (near-black #0a0a0b background), minimalist, premium feel inspired by Apple and Linear. SF Pro font family. Emerald/green accent color (#34d399) used for CTAs, highlights, and branding. Generous whitespace between sections (~140px gaps). Subtle film grain texture overlaid on the entire page.

**Signature visual element:** Procedurally animated silk fabric rendered on HTML canvas. Flowing emerald-green vertical pleats with specular highlights, rendered at low resolution and 60% opacity for a subtle, atmospheric background effect. Applied behind the hero section, pricing section, and final CTA section. The animation is slow and gentle — more ambient texture than attention-grabbing.

**Color-coded system throughout:** Red = warnings/problems/deep work, Amber = caution/testimonials, Violet = features/focus hours, Emerald = positive/earnings/CTAs.

**Scroll reveal animations:** All sections fade in from below (30px translate + opacity) as they enter the viewport.

## Emotional Arc of the Page

1. **Confrontation** (Hero) — "You know you're wasting time." Direct, no sugar-coating.
2. **Evidence** (Stats) — 23 min refocus time, 1,200 app switches, 720 hours lost. Research-backed shock.
3. **Guilt/Recognition** (Problem Cards) — "The Rabbit Hole," "Self-Sabotage," "5pm Guilt" — the reader sees themselves.
4. **Absolution** (Pivot) — "It's not a discipline problem. It's a tools problem." Shifts blame from user to environment.
5. **Social Proof** (Testimonials) — Other people like you had the same problem and solved it.
6. **Clarity** (How It Works) — Three concrete steps: Plan, Focus, Earn. Interactive mockups show exactly what happens.
7. **Differentiation** (Features + Comparison) — Why this is not another blocklist. Side-by-side competitive table.
8. **Trust** (Accountability + FAQ) — Addresses objections: privacy, browser support, performance, bypass-resistance.
9. **Value** (Pricing) — Anchored to cost of distraction ($0.20/day vs 720 hours lost), not compared to competitors.
10. **Action** (Final CTA) — Identity-affirming close: "The person you want to be is on the other side of one download."

## Key Differentiators Communicated

1. **AI relevance scoring** — not blocklists, the AI understands what you're working on
2. **Progressive enforcement** — nudge → grayscale → block, never punished without warning
3. **Earned browse system** — focus earns social media time, turns distraction into a reward
4. **Accountability partner + Strict Mode** — survives force-quit, needs partner's code to disable
5. **All data on-device** — AI runs locally, nothing leaves the Mac
6. **AI day planner** — Quick Plan builds a schedule in under a minute
