Product teardown — why opening the app gives you nothing

2026-06-11 night · Evidence: live host app (Ameer's real data, dev build w/ today's fixes) + fresh-account VM walk. All screenshots in docs/teardown-2026-06-11/. Companion to the onboarding cold-walk report (which covered minutes 0–5; this covers day 2+).
Thesis: the app now has a great first five minutes and a decent settings room, but no daily loop. The new onboarding takes a fired-up stranger from pain → picks → deal → a running session. Then tomorrow they open the app and land on a Today tab that is structurally empty: no greeting, no question, no record of yesterday, no next action. Every reason to come back lives in surfaces that don't exist yet (daily commitment, timeline, review). The "vibe-coded" feel is mostly four specific, fixable classes of defect: dead primary surface, contradictory state, silent protection failure, and inconsistent vocabulary.

Exhibit A — Today, the primary tab, on a real heavy-use account (8:40 PM)

Ameer's actual Today at 8:40 PM after a full day on the machine: "No goals for this week yet", an empty schedule grid, a red now-line over nothing. Bottom-left says "Free time" while the sidebar simultaneously offers "⏹ Stop session".

This single screen is the product's core failure. The ADHD user opens the app with exactly one question — "what should I be doing right now?" — and this screen answers nothing. Specifically:

#DefectClassDetail
A1No "now" cardMISSINGNothing states the current mode (focusing? free? what session? since when?). The onboarding's screen-10 question ("Pick one thing to finish today") has no daily equivalent — it was the best screen in the product and it shows exactly once, ever.
A2State contradiction on screenBROKEN"Free time" label and "⏹ Stop session" visible at the same moment. One of them is lying. Trust dies here.
A3Empty timeline carries no historyMISSINGThe schedule grid shows only future-planning. A full day of real usage (TimeTracker has the data!) renders as blank rows. Your May 18 ask — "track how I'm actually spending my time and present it on the timeline" — is precisely the missing organ. An evening user should see their day filled in, good and bad.
A4"No goals for this week yet" on an account with 14 goalsBROKENPartly the (now fixed) ISO8601 decode bug, partly weekly-goal filtering: old goals have no week assigned, so the strip is empty and scolds a power user. Empty-state copy punishes instead of helping.
A5Visual hierarchy absentMISSINGA thin content column floats in a huge dark void; sidebar and canvas don't compose; nothing is the hero. Reads as wireframe, not product.
