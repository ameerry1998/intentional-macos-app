Frequently asked questions

What if I need YouTube for work?

That's exactly the problem this solves. The AI scores each page against your task. A React tutorial? Relevant. A compilation video? Nudged back. Same site, different verdicts.

Can I really not turn it off?

With Strict Mode, the app survives Cmd+Q, relaunches on reboot, and needs your partner's 6-digit code to disable. Without it, you can quit anytime.

Is my data private?

Completely. All AI runs on-device. Your page titles, URLs, and usage data never leave your Mac.

Which browsers work?

Chrome, Safari, Arc, Brave, Edge, Firefox, Opera, and Vivaldi. Desktop apps are monitored natively too.

Does it slow down my Mac?

No. AI runs on Apple's Neural Engine. Tab polling is every 10 seconds. You won't notice it.

How is this different from Freedom?

Freedom uses blocklists — every site is allowed or blocked. Intentional uses AI to understand context, plus earned browse rewards and progressive enforcement. It's not a wall. It's a system.
