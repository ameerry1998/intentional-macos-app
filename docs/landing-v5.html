Frequently asked questions

Can I really not turn it off?

Up to you. Without Strict Mode, quit whenever you want. With it on? It survives Cmd+Q, relaunches on reboot, and needs your partner's 6-digit code to disable. You pick which version of yourself makes the rules.

Is my data private?

Everything stays on your Mac. The AI runs locally on Apple's Neural Engine. Your URLs, page titles, and browsing data never leave the device. No cloud. No account. No tracking.

Which browsers does it work with?

Chrome, Safari, Arc, Brave, Edge, Firefox, Opera, and Vivaldi. Desktop apps — Slack, Figma, Xcode, whatever — are tracked natively too.

Does it slow down my Mac?

Nope. AI runs on Apple's Neural Engine. Tab checks happen every 10 seconds. You'll forget it's running.

How does the AI know what's relevant?

It reads your tab title and checks it against whatever you said you're working on. Figma tutorial while you're designing? Fine. Figma tutorial while you're supposed to be writing? Caught. It runs entirely on your Mac — no cloud, no one watching.

What if I work from multiple devices?

Right now, Intentional is macOS only. Your Mac is where the deep work happens — and where the distractions live. iOS and Windows are on the roadmap, but we're focused on making one platform bulletproof before we spread thin.

What happens if I need to take a break?

Schedule a Free Time block. Your earned browse balance carries over, social media is unlocked, and nothing gets flagged. Intentional knows the difference between a break and a spiral. You planned the break — enjoy it.
